Strawberry Milkshake

Prep Time: 10 mins

style=float: rightyield: 4

Ingredients




steps

  1. Blend half of strawberries, 1/4 tsp. Vanilla, 1 cup ice cream and 2 tsp. milk in blender unit smooth.
  2. Pour into 2 glasses. Repeat with remianing strawberries, ice cream and milk. Serve immediately.
