Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Prep Time:10 minutes

Yield: 4

Ingredients





steps

  1. Stir together butter and parmesan cheese in a small bowl.
  2. Spread 1 1/2 tsp. butter mixture on 1 side of each bread slices. Place 4 bread slices, buttered sides doown, on a wax paper. Top with provolone and mozzarella cheese; top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides up
  3. Spread 1 1/2 tsp. butter, on a hot giddle or in a non-stick skillet over medium heart, gently pressing with a spatula, 4 minuteson each side or until golden breown and cheese is melted
